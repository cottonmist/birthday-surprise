For You ❤️

I long for the day when it’s just you and me, and in the darkness of the night, I drift to sleep on your chest, your heartbeat becoming the only clock I listen to.
I don’t just want your time; I want your thoughts. I want to be the ghost that haunts the back of your mind until you can’t remember what it was like to be alone.
Happy 17th, my love. They say seventeen is a year of change, but my obsession with you is the only thing that never does. March 15 belongs to you — but you, every breath and every year, belong to me. I love you more than you’ll ever be able to handle.
